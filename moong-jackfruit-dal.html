Why does this work so well?

Dry-roasted moong brings a toasty, nutty backbone. Young jackfruit soaks up spices and takes on a meaty, pulled texture. Mustard oil provides sharp heat; a spoon of ghee at the end adds silk. Together they do something greater than the sum of their parts.
